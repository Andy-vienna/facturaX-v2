selfsigned KeyStore erzeugen (hier: 1440 Tage gültig):
------------------------------------------------------
keytool -genkey -keyalg RSA -alias selfsigned -keystore keystore.jks -storepass password -validity 1440 -keysize 2048 -ext SAN=IP:192.168.1.60

server.cert exportieren für App:
--------------------------------
keytool -exportcert -alias selfsigned -keystore keystore.jks -rfc -file server.crt -storepass password
